Supplemental Personal Information Processing Consent for Xiaohongshu International E-Commerce Services

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

This Supplemental Consent is part of and supplements the Xiaohongshu User Privacy Policy. It applies specifically to users who use the international cross-border e-commerce shopping services available on the Xiaohongshu Platform. In the event of any inconsistency between this Supplemental Consent and the main Privacy Policy regarding international e-commerce services, this Supplemental Consent shall prevail.

I. Scope of Application

This Supplemental Consent applies to you if you use the Xiaohongshu Platform to:

  • Browse, purchase, or receive cross-border goods or services shipped from overseas.
  • Make payments in foreign currencies or through international payment channels.
  • Provide shipping addresses located outside Mainland China.
  • Use any other feature designated as part of Xiaohongshu International E-Commerce Services.

II. Additional Personal Information We Collect

In addition to the information described in the main Privacy Policy, when you use International E-Commerce Services, we may collect the following additional categories of personal information:

1. Identity Verification for Customs Clearance

For cross-border transactions, applicable customs regulations in China and the destination country may require us to collect and submit identity verification information. This may include:

  • Your full legal name (as it appears on your government-issued identity document).
  • Identity document type and number (e.g., national ID card number, passport number).
  • Photos of both sides of your identity document.
  • Tax identification number (where required by the destination country).

This information is collected solely for the purpose of customs declaration and clearance and is shared only with customs authorities, logistics providers, and other parties required by law to facilitate the import of goods.

2. Shipping and Delivery Information

For international delivery, we collect the recipient's full name, complete international shipping address (including country, region, postal code), contact telephone number, and email address.

3. International Payment Information

When making payments for international orders, we may collect your international payment account information, including foreign currency credit or debit card details (processed securely through certified payment processors), international bank account information (where applicable), and billing address.

4. Import Duties and Tax Information

In some jurisdictions, we are required to collect and report information about the value and nature of goods being imported. We may collect information about the declared value of goods and applicable tariff codes.

III. How We Use This Additional Information

Information Category Purpose of Use Legal Basis
Identity documents for customs Customs declaration and clearance; compliance with import regulations Legal obligation; contract performance
International shipping address Delivery of purchased goods; logistics coordination Contract performance
International payment details Processing cross-border payments; fraud prevention Contract performance; legitimate interests
Import duty and tax information Compliance with import tax regulations; customs filing Legal obligation

IV. Cross-Border Transfer of Personal Information

Due to the nature of international e-commerce, your personal information will necessarily be transferred across borders. Specifically:

  • Your shipping address and identity information will be shared with logistics providers and customs authorities in the destination country.
  • Your payment information will be processed by international payment networks operating across multiple countries.
  • Your order information may be shared with overseas merchants fulfilling your order.

By using International E-Commerce Services and placing a cross-border order, you expressly authorize these cross-border transfers. We will take appropriate contractual and technical safeguards to protect your personal information during transfer, in accordance with the requirements of the Xiaohongshu User Privacy Policy.

V. Sharing with Third Parties

In addition to the sharing described in the main Privacy Policy, we share your personal information with the following additional categories of third parties in connection with International E-Commerce Services:

  • International logistics and courier companies: to arrange delivery and customs clearance of your orders.
  • Overseas merchants and suppliers: to fulfill your orders, process returns, and provide after-sales service.
  • Customs and border protection authorities: in the destination country or origin country, as required by applicable customs regulations.
  • International payment processors and financial institutions: to process your cross-border payment transactions.
  • Tax authorities: where required by law to report import duties or value-added tax.

VI. Retention of Additional Information

Identity documents and customs-related information collected for cross-border transactions are retained for the period required by applicable customs regulations in China and the destination country, which is typically no less than three (3) years from the date of transaction. Payment information is retained in accordance with financial record-keeping requirements, typically five (5) years. All other additional information is retained in accordance with the main Privacy Policy.

VII. Your Rights

You have the same rights with respect to the additional personal information collected under this Supplemental Consent as described in Section IV of the main Privacy Policy. To exercise your rights, please contact us at shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com. Please note that certain information (such as identity documents for customs purposes) may not be deletable if required for ongoing legal compliance obligations.

VIII. Contact Us

For any questions regarding the processing of your personal information in connection with Xiaohongshu International E-Commerce Services, please contact us at: shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com.
