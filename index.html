I. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

The Xiaohongshu Platform is a lifestyle platform for young people. We will collect and use the personal information you provide voluntarily or that is generated during your use of our products and/or services, following the principles of legitimacy, legality, and necessity.

1. Account Registration and Login

When you register and log in, we will collect your mobile phone number, password, or verification code. You can also choose to fill in your avatar, name, gender, birthday, occupation, region, and profile information. If you log in with a third-party account, we will additionally collect your mobile phone number to complete real-name verification.

2. Providing Products and/or Services to You

2.1 Information Browsing, Posting, Liking, Collecting, and Sharing

To provide core information display services and personalized recommendations, we collect your gender, birthday, browsing records, and click operation records. When you use posting and editing functions, we request authorization for storage, camera, and microphone permissions.

2.2 Friend Recommendation or Discovery

With your express consent, we will encrypt and upload your address book information to our servers to recommend friends. You can turn this off in [Me] - [Settings] - [Privacy Settings] - [Hide Me from People I May Know].

2.3 Secure Operation and Risk Control

To ensure safe operation, we collect device information (including device model, device name, device unique identifiers such as IMEI, Android ID, IDFA, IDFV, OAID), running processes, network device hardware address (MAC Address), IP Address, and software usage records for security risk control verification.

2.4 Search Function

When you use the search function, we collect your search keywords, browsing records, access time, and interaction records to provide relevant content and improve our services.

2.5 Location-Based Services

When you enable device location and use location-based services, we collect and use your location information. You can stop collection by turning off location services in your device's system settings.

2.6 Interactive Features and Services

When you follow accounts, browse, comment, like, collect or share content, we collect the relevant interaction records. Live stream hosts' and viewers' nicknames, avatars, and interaction information will be publicly displayed in live stream rooms.

2.7 Virtual Assets

[Wallet] records your charge, balance and usage information to allow you to check your virtual assets.

2.8 Real-Name Authentication

When you apply for identity authentication, live stream functions, or account retrieval, we collect your real name, ID number or photo(s), and facial recognition information to verify your identity as required by law.

2.9 Product Purchase

When you place an order, we collect your transaction information, order number, order time, transaction amount, payment method, recipient's name, address, contact number, and logistics information.

2.10 Customer Services and After-Sales

When you contact customer service or submit after-sales applications, we collect communication records and relevant account and order information to resolve your issues. Calls with customer service staff may be recorded for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes.

2.11 Payment

During the payment process, we collect your third-party payment accounts (Alipay, WeChat, UnionPay and related accounts).

2.12 Information Pre-Fill

To save you time, we automatically pre-fill form information based on information you previously provided during registration or purchasing.

2.13 Skin Test and Makeup Trial

When you use these functions, we collect camera permission or facial recognition information with your individual consent. We will not store your facial recognition information after providing these functions.

2.14 Generative Artificial Intelligence Services

When you use AI services (including AskNow, Dots, AI Pet, AI Photo Retouching), we collect text, images, voice, and behavioral information you input, as well as your feedback. De-identified data may be used for model training and optimization. You may contact us to withdraw consent for this use.

2.15 Widget and Desktop Interactions

When you add Xiaohongshu desktop widgets or use device OS interactive functions, we may utilize auto-start capabilities to ensure timely delivery of information through these components.

2.16 Push Notifications

To enable timely push notifications, we obtain power-on broadcast permission, use a third-party message push service to generate a device identification ID, and read your phone status to determine whether the app is in the foreground or background.

2.17 Personalized Content

To show you personalized content, we collect search/play/display records, audio and video playback records, and device sensor information. You can turn off personalized content in [Me] - [Settings] - [Privacy Settings] - [Personalization Options].

2.18 Advertising and Commercial Information

Based on your device information, browsing and usage data, and purchase history, we generate population profiles based on characteristic tags to display and push personalized advertisements. You can unsubscribe from commercial messages through SMS prompts or by contacting us.

2.19 Clipboard Access

When you share or receive shared information or participate in activities, we access your clipboard to obtain tokens, sharing codes or links to enable sharing and activity participation.

3. Personal Information Collected from Third Parties

If you use a third-party platform account to log in, after you explicitly authorize the account binding, the third-party platform will provide your avatar, nickname, region (if any), gender (if any), and friend information to us so that you can directly log in and use the Xiaohongshu Platform.

4. Exceptions to Obtaining Authorized Consent

According to relevant laws and regulations, we may collect and use some necessary personal information without your authorized consent when: (1) necessary for the conclusion or performance of a contract; (2) necessary for statutory duties or obligations; (3) necessary to respond to public health emergencies or to protect life, health, and property safety in emergencies; (4) for news reporting or public interest acts within a reasonable scope; (5) processing personal information that is publicly disclosed within a reasonable extent; (6) other circumstances provided by laws and regulations.

II. How We Share, Transfer and Publicly Disclose Your Personal Information

1. Sharing

We will not share your personal information with any company, organization or individual except with your prior consent, at your request, or when necessary to share with our business partners. Our business partners include:

  • Third-party merchants on the Xiaohongshu Platform, for order fulfillment and after-sales services.
  • Third-party logistics providers, who receive delivery information solely for the purpose of completing delivery.
  • Advertising and analytics partners, who receive de-identified and aggregated data only.
  • Content review service providers, to ensure platform content safety.
  • Real-name authentication providers, to verify identity for account retrieval.
  • Skin test function providers, to deliver the skin test and makeup trial features.
  • Third-party SDK service providers, including one-click login, push notifications, location services, cloud storage, account security, payment, and live stream services. Please refer to our Third Party Information Sharing List for details.

2. Transfer

We will not transfer your personal information to any other company, organization or individual without your express consent, except with your prior consent, at your request, or in the event of a merger, division, dissolution or bankruptcy where we will inform you of the recipient's name and contact information and require the recipient to continue complying with this Privacy Policy.

3. Public Disclosure

We will not publicly disclose your personal information except to display necessary desensitized information in prize or punishment notices, or with your express consent.

4. Exceptions for Sharing, Transfer and Public Disclosure

We may share, transfer or publicly disclose your personal information without prior authorization when: (1) necessary for a contract; (2) necessary for statutory duties; (3) necessary in public health emergencies; (4) for public interest acts; (5) processing publicly disclosed information within a reasonable extent; (6) other circumstances provided by laws and regulations.

III. How We Store and Protect Your Personal Information

1. Storage

Personal information obtained during the operation of the Xiaohongshu Platform is stored within the territory of the People's Republic of China. Cross-border transfers occur only when expressly required by applicable laws, with your express authorization, or through your own cross-border transactions. We will store your personal information within a reasonable and necessary period as required by law, after which we will delete or anonymize it.

2. Protection

We adopt various security measures in line with industry standards to protect your personal information against destruction, theft, leakage, unauthorized access, use, disclosure and alteration. We have established data classification and grading systems, data security management standards, and data security development standards. All accounts are equipped with security protection functions. In the event of a security incident, we will promptly notify you by email, letter, phone, push notification, or public announcement as required by law.

IV. How You Manage Your Personal Information

1. Self-determination to Receive Information

You can choose to follow or unfollow accounts based on your preferences.

2. Right to Access, Rectify and Delete

You can access, correct and delete relevant personal information through:

  • [Me - Edit Profile Information] for avatar, nickname, gender, region, birthday and profile.
  • [Me] – [Settings] – [Account Security] for mobile phone number, login password and bound third-party accounts.
  • [Me] – [Settings] – [Privacy Settings] for comment settings, address book recommendations, and location information.
  • [Me - Posts/Collects/Likes] for your posted and collected content.

We shall delete your personal information, or you have the right to request deletion, when: (1) the purpose of processing has been achieved or is no longer necessary; (2) we have stopped providing services or the storage period has expired; (3) you have withdrawn your consent; (4) we are found to have processed your personal information in violation of laws or agreements; (5) other circumstances provided by laws.

3. Change or Withdrawal of Authorization

You may change or withdraw authorization scope through [Me] - Settings - Notification Settings / Privacy Settings / General Settings], or by adjusting device system permissions (microphone, address book, camera, location, storage, etc.). Withdrawing authorization will not affect our previous processing based on your prior consent.

4. Restraint of Automated Decision Making

Where automated decisions significantly affect your legal rights and interests, you have the right to ask us to explain, and we will provide appropriate remedies.

V. How You Cancel Your Account

You may cancel your account through [Me] – Settings – Help Center – [Community Questions – How to Cancel Account] or [Me] - Settings - Account Security – Account Cancellation]. We will process your cancellation within 7 working days after receiving your application and verifying your identity. After cancellation, your personal information will be removed from the Xiaohongshu Platform.

Any use of third-party services (including any personal information you provide to the third party) shall be subject to the terms of service and privacy policy of that third party, not this Privacy Policy. Xiaohongshu shall not bear any legal liability for third parties' use of your personal information.

VII. How We Use Cookies and Other Similar Technologies

When you accept cookies, the Xiaohongshu Platform will set or retrieve cookies on your computer and related mobile devices so that you can log in or use Platform Services that rely on cookies, and to provide more personalized services including promotional services. You have the right to accept or decline cookies by modifying your browser settings or setting it on your mobile device. However, declining cookies may prevent you from logging in or using certain Platform services or functions.

VIII. Protection of Minors

If you are under the age of 18, please use the Xiaohongshu Platform only under the supervision and guidance of your Guardian, and only after ensuring your Guardian agrees to the content of this Privacy Policy. We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. If we find that we have collected minors' personal information without the prior and verifiable consent of their parents or Guardians, we will endeavor to delete the relevant data as soon as possible.

We have designed and developed a "Minor Mode". Guardians can enable it and set a password via [Settings - Minor Mode]. Please refer to our Rules Regarding the Protection of Personal Information of Minors for more details.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in accordance with new products and relevant laws and regulations. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your express consent. We will notify you of any updates via the Xiaohongshu Platform push notification or other reasonable means.
