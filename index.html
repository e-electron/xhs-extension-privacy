I. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

The Xiaohongshu Platform is a lifestyle platform for young people, with the mission to "inspire lives to share and discover the wonders of the world". Users can record moments of their lives and share their lifestyles through short videos, pictures, texts and other forms of content, and form interactions based on interests. In this Privacy Policy, we will explain the scope of personal information that each function of our products and/or services may collect, the purposes of collection, the methods of collection, and the possible consequences of not providing your personal information. Please be aware that you can still use other functions of the Xiaohongshu Platform Services if you do not provide the corresponding information necessary for each function.

We will collect and use the personal information you provide voluntarily or that is generated during your use of our products and/or services, following the principles of legitimacy, legality, and necessity, for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy. If we intend to use your personal information for purposes not specified in this Privacy Policy, or use the collected information for other purposes based on specific needs, we will inform you in a reasonable manner and obtain your consent again before using it. You can access the details through [Me - Settings - Personal Information List].

1. Account Registration and Login

1.1 "Xiaohongshu Account" or "Account" means the account that Users may need to register when using the Xiaohongshu Platform. When you register and log in, we will collect your mobile phone number, password, or verification code for the purpose of registering and logging into your account. If you use the one-click login function, based on our cooperation with the telecom operators, we will collect your mobile phone number and network operator information to provide you with fast login services. Additionally, to help you complete the registration process, we will also collect your gender, interests, and age to provide you with initial Xiaohongshu Platform services. If you do not wish to provide such information, you can click [ ... ] - [Skip this step] at the top right of the collection page.

If your mobile phone number cannot be used for account login or verification due to reasons such as service suspension, deactivation, or reassignment, to confirm that the relevant operation is initiated by you personally and to ensure account security, we will provide your account-bound mobile phone number, the real-name information you fill in during verification, or other personal mobile phone numbers to the telecommunications operator and/or its authorized partners. The telecommunications operator will use communication-related data to assess the security of this operation.

When you use the account recovery or phone number replacement function, we will collect your full name, ID number, currently used mobile phone number, and previously used mobile phone numbers, and provide such information to the telecommunications operator to verify whether the mobile phone number bound to your account at registration is used by you personally. The operator will feedback the verification result to us.

1.2 If you log in with a third-party account, to meet the requirements of the network real-name system, we will additionally collect your mobile phone number to complete real-name verification before you can use functions and services that require real-name verification, such as posting and commenting. Please consider carefully before providing such information. If you refuse to provide it, you may not be able to use the corresponding functions normally.

1.3 You can also choose to fill in or change your avatar, name, gender, birthday, occupation, region, school, profile, and outfit information according to your needs to complete your information. However, if you do not provide this information, it will not affect your use of this product and the related services.

1.4 If you successfully register a Xiaohongshu Account, we will display your name, avatar, region, and profile information on the Xiaohongshu Platform.

2.1 Information Browsing, Posting, Liking, Collecting, and Sharing Functions

2.1.1 To provide you with our core information display services and recommend notes and related information that may interest you, we will collect the personal information you fill in, your gender, birthday, browsing records (your follows, browsing), and click operation records (interests, clicks, searches, likes, collections, shares, comments, posting records, and related behaviors). 2.1.2 When you use the posting and editing content functions, we will request your authorization for storage (photo albums, media, and other files), camera, and microphone (recording) permissions. When you click "➕" in app front page, if you have authorized our access to photo album in your device, we will preload the photo album information while all the preload information will only be cached in your device temporarily and will not be uploaded to our server.

To improve video upload speed, enrich posting functions, and optimize the experience, we will temporarily load the video you edited to the server before you click "publish notes". If you exit the editing page or do not post the note for other reasons, we will immediately delete the related video. You can disable this function through [Me] – [Settings] – [General Settings] – [Not allow Pre-upload videos before publishing].

Please note that to protect your privacy, if the map accuracy level is too high, we will not display your live stream information on the map.
