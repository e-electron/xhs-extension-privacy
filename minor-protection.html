Rules Regarding the Protection of Personal Information of Minors of Xiaohongshu

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

These Rules are supplemental to the Xiaohongshu User Privacy Policy and specifically govern the collection, use, storage, and protection of personal information of users under the age of 18.

I. Introduction

Xiaohongshu is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of minors. We recognize that minors require additional protections, and we have designed these Rules to address the specific needs of minors and their guardians in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, including the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China and the Provisions on the Protection of Personal Information of Minors Online.

"Minor" refers to any user under the age of 18. "Guardian" refers to the legal guardian of a minor, including parents or other persons lawfully appointed to act in that capacity.

II. Guardian Consent

If a user is a minor, the use of Xiaohongshu Platform Services must be done with the knowledge and verifiable consent of the minor's Guardian. By allowing a minor to use Xiaohongshu, the Guardian acknowledges and agrees to these Rules and the Xiaohongshu User Privacy Policy on behalf of the minor.

Where required by applicable laws, we will take reasonable steps to verify that consent has been obtained from a Guardian before collecting or processing personal information of a minor under the age of 14.

III. Information We Collect from Minors

We apply the principle of minimum necessary data collection to minors. We collect only the personal information strictly necessary to provide the requested service. This includes:

  • Account registration information (mobile phone number or email address, username).
  • Age or date of birth (to determine appropriate content and feature access).
  • Content created and interactions initiated by the minor on the platform.
  • Device and technical information necessary for platform operation and security.

We do not collect the following categories of information from minors unless strictly necessary and with explicit Guardian consent:

  • Precise geolocation data.
  • Facial recognition information.
  • Contact list or address book data.
  • Behavioral profiling data used for targeted advertising.

IV. Minor Mode

We have designed and developed a dedicated "Minor Mode" for the Xiaohongshu Platform. Guardians or minors can enable Minor Mode through [Settings - Minor Mode] and set a passcode to prevent unauthorized changes.

When Minor Mode is enabled:

  • Daily usage time is limited in accordance with applicable regulations.
  • Content recommendations are filtered to show only age-appropriate content.
  • Certain features (such as live streaming, in-app purchases, and direct messaging with strangers) are restricted or disabled.
  • Personalized advertising based on behavioral tracking is disabled.
  • The minor's account will not appear in friend recommendations to other users.

V. How We Use Minors' Personal Information

We use personal information collected from minors solely to:

  • Create and maintain the minor's account.
  • Provide platform features and services appropriate for minors.
  • Ensure the safety and security of the minor's account.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Communicate with the Guardian regarding account activity, where necessary.

We do not use minors' personal information for targeted advertising or to build behavioral profiles for commercial purposes.

VI. Sharing of Minors' Personal Information

We apply stricter standards when sharing personal information of minors. We will not share minors' personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We share minors' personal information with third parties only when:

  • The Guardian has provided explicit consent.
  • It is necessary for the provision of a specific service requested by the minor or Guardian.
  • Required by law or to protect the safety of the minor.

VII. Guardian Rights and Controls

Guardians have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about their child.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete the minor's account and associated personal information.
  • Restrict certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for the collection and use of their child's personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, Guardians may contact us at shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com. We will verify the Guardian's identity before processing the request and respond within fifteen working days.

VIII. Storage and Security of Minors' Personal Information

We store minors' personal information within the territory of the People's Republic of China. We implement enhanced security measures for minors' personal information, including stricter access controls, additional encryption, and more frequent security audits. We retain minors' personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by applicable law.

IX. Reporting Concerns

If you are a Guardian and have concerns about your child's use of Xiaohongshu, or if you believe that we have collected personal information from a minor without appropriate consent, please contact us promptly at shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com. We will investigate and take appropriate action.

X. Updates to These Rules

We may update these Rules from time to time in accordance with changes to applicable laws and regulations or our platform. We will notify Guardians of material changes through in-app notifications or other reasonable means. Continued use of the platform by a minor after notification of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Rules by the Guardian.

XI. Contact Us

For any questions regarding the protection of minors' personal information, please contact us at: shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com.
