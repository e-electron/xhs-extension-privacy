rednote Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy applies to users of "rednote" — the overseas-operated version of the Xiaohongshu platform — who have registered accounts outside Mainland China. "rednote," "we," "us," or "our" refers to Xingin Information Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. and its affiliates operating the rednote platform internationally.

If you are a Mainland China user, please refer to the Xiaohongshu User Privacy Policy.

By using rednote, you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

I. Information We Collect

1. Account Registration

When you register for a rednote account, we collect your email address or mobile phone number, username, and password. You may optionally provide your profile photo, display name, gender, date of birth, and bio.

2. Content You Create

We collect the content you create and share on rednote, including photos, videos, text, audio, comments, likes, and shares. We also collect metadata associated with this content, such as timestamps and device information.

3. Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you interact with rednote, including browsing history, search queries, content you view or interact with, features you use, and the time, frequency and duration of your activities.

4. Device and Technical Information

We collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID, device ID), IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device model, network type, and diagnostic data such as crash reports and performance logs.

5. Location Information

With your permission, we collect precise geolocation data from your device. We also derive approximate location from your IP address to provide location-based features such as local content recommendations.

6. Communications

We collect the content of messages you send and receive on rednote, including direct messages and comments. We also collect your contact list if you grant us permission to access it for the purpose of finding friends on rednote.

II. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • To provide, personalize, and improve rednote features and content.
  • To recommend content, accounts, and products that may interest you.
  • To enable you to interact with other users and share content.
  • To process transactions and send related information, including purchase confirmations and invoices.
  • To send push notifications, in-app messages, and other communications about your account and platform updates.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
  • To conduct analytics and research to understand how rednote is used and how to improve it.

III. How We Share Your Information

1. With Other Users

Your profile information, posts, and interactions may be visible to other rednote users depending on your privacy settings.

2. With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics, content moderation, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as directed by us.

3. For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of rednote, our users, or others.

4. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

IV. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside, including China, Singapore, and the United States. We take appropriate measures — such as standard contractual clauses — to ensure your personal information remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy when transferred internationally.

V. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with rednote services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it.

VI. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, to restrict or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, please contact us at shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

VII. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide and improve rednote services, to authenticate users, to remember your preferences, and to deliver personalized content and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect the functionality of rednote.

VIII. Children's Privacy

rednote is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

IX. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

X. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the rednote app or by other reasonable means. Your continued use of rednote after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

XI. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: shuduizhang@xiaohongshu.com.
